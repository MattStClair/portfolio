Bio

Hello. My name is Matt St.Clair. I am an Air Force veteran, mental health therapist at Centered Mind Counseling, and a freelance web developer. Currently, I am working with Thero.org, to blend my skills as a developer and mental health professional. After my graduate school program in psychology, I completed a B.S. in computer applications development from Renton Technical College and full stack web development courses at Codefellows code camp in Seattle. I enjoy front end as well as back end work but am especially fascinated with UX design and how it blends with my social sciences background. I also enjoy front-end work because it allows me to collaborate and create with clients. I also enjoy fishing and free parking.
